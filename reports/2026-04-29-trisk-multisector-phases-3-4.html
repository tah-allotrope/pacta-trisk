7. Limitations / Second-Best Alternative

  • Cement and steel still rely on sector-level SDA context rather than borrower-level SDA alignment.
  • The dashboard now consumes the new manifest-backed layout, but phase 5 still needs end-to-end documentation and broader manual validation.
  • Second-best alternative not chosen: keep a flat snapshot root and add sector prefixes to filenames. That would make the loader and page logic more brittle than a folder-plus-manifest contract.
